We have a daily BTC-USD csv charts at the moment with the columns

* Data
* Open
* High
* Low
* Adjusted Close
* Close
* Volume

== Goal

The goal is to have a comprehensive and scalable data structure of different cryptocurrencies and be able to put them in our database
